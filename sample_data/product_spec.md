## Features

### SmartGrid Weighing Pan
The SmartGrid weighing pan reduces the effects of air turbulence by allowing
airflow to pass through the grid structure, resulting in faster stabilization
and more repeatable results.

### StatusLight™
An LED indicator ring on the terminal provides an immediate visual cue that the
balance is ready for use, showing green when the reading is stable and red during
stabilization.

### Automatic Internal Calibration (FACT)
Fully Automatic Calibration Technology (FACT) automatically calibrates the
balance based on predefined temperature criteria, ensuring accuracy without
operator intervention.

### Electrostatic Charge Detection
The built-in StaticDetect™ sensor warns operators when electrostatic charges on
samples are likely to cause weighing errors, and suggests corrective actions.
